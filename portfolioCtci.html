Project Scope

" 行動簽核 "
" 我要找人 "

The myCTCI App is an internal management tool designed for employees, offering features such as business trip and leave request management, mobile approval for leave, travel, and overtime applications, as well as employee directory search, capital information lookup, direct calling, and meeting scheduling. These features aim to enhance organizational management efficiency, create a highly productive work environment, and reduce communication costs.

After completing the initial design, we identified several areas for improvement and optimization, which led to the initiation of this Redesign project.

Design Goals ⤵

INTUITIVE NAVIGATION

Simple and intuitive workflows that reduce information overload, enabling users to operate effortlessly.

A MINIMALIST VISUAL STYLE

Enhanced aesthetics by combining soft color tones with corporate branding colors to emphasize key information.

Optimized information display

Integration of features and pages with a focus on key content, clear information hierarchy, and improved readability and clarity.

Wireframes ⤵

Provide an outline structure that clearly illustrates the app's layout and organization, serving as a visual guide for design and development.

Design System ⤵

Following the corporate branding guidelines, yellow and gray are used as the primary colors.
The design direction continues to emphasize functionality and usability as its core principles. Aesthetic elements incorporate soft color tones combined with corporate branding colors to enhance the presentation of key information. The overall positioning is efficient, streamlined, and cohesive.

Overview ⤵

Streamlined Approvals with One-Click and Batch Processing.

Simplify approvals with one-click functionality, making the process fast and efficient. Complete all workflows with ease, reducing complexity and minimizing errors. Support actions like approve, reject, add countersignatures, or reassign tasks, ensuring a more flexible and efficient approval process. The system automatically categorizes pending items and organizes them by submission time, enabling quick access to key documents and significantly improving administrative efficiency.

Instant Voice Connection for Enhanced Collaboration.

Simply connect via a mobile device or computer to make voice calls, reducing communication costs while enabling real-time team interactions. Whether discussing critical details or seeking support from teammates, the system offers reminders and confirmation features to ensure an efficient and seamless communication process, making collaboration smoother than ever.
